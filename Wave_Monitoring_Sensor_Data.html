Wave Monitoring on Spearhead Lake


Update: August 18, 2022

Our prototype wave monitoring device has been successfully deployed at Spearhead Lake. The senors used in this device include: (from bottom up) ultrasonic rangefinder (records the distance to the water surface), temperature sensor (records the temperature and humidity of the air), control box (stores the battery and other electronics used to collect and transfer the data), solar panel (charges the battery), and anemometer (records the wind speed and direction). We are currently working on processing the data for display. The current plots include data recorded on a single day.
